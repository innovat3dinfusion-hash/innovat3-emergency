What is Innovat3? A smart NFC + QR emergency safety tag worn on the body. When scanned by any smartphone (no app needed), it instantly displays the wearer's emergency profile and sends a real-time GPS alert to nominated family members via WhatsApp and Telegram. Made in South Africa. Ideal for children, hikers, elderly individuals, and persons with disabilities.
