A few thoughts that may help: 1. PARENT-FUNDED MODEL: Many schools run this as a parent opt-in programme rather than a school expense. Parents pay for their child's tag directly (R199 hardware + R49/month). The school simply facilitates distribution. Zero cost to the school. 2. PILOT COST: A 25-tag pilot costs R3,975 in hardware (once-off) + R975/month. Split across 25 families, that's R159 hardware + R39/month per child — less than a takeaway meal. 3. SGB FUNDRAISER: Some schools have used tag sales as a minor fundraiser — buying at bulk rate and selling to parents at the standard rate, retaining the margin for the school fund. 📧
Always send from info@innovat3.co.za — not a personal Gmail. Looks professional and matches your website domain.
📎
Always attach the pricing PDF to the first email — principals forward it to their deputy and SGB without asking you for it.
⏰
Best time to send: Tuesday–Thursday, 07:00–08:30 (before assembly) or 14:00–15:30 (after school). Avoid Mondays and Fridays.
📱
If no email reply after follow-up — call the school reception and ask to speak to the principal's PA. Offer to WhatsApp the pricing sheet directly.
🎯
Target Grade R–3 first — younger children are the strongest use case and parents of young children are most emotionally motivated to buy.
