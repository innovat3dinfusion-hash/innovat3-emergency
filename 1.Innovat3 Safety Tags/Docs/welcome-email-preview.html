Getting Started

1
Attach your tag
Attach the tag to wherever the person you registered spends most of their time:
• Key ring or bag strap
• Pet collar or harness
• Vehicle key chain
• Child's school bag or lunchbox
2
Test your emergency card
Tap your NFC tag with any smartphone, or click the button below to open your card. Confirm your name, photo, blood type and emergency contacts all appear correctly. If anything needs updating, contact us.
3
Set up GPS alerts on Telegram
To receive instant GPS alerts when your tag is scanned:
1. Download the Telegram app on your phone
2. Search for @innovat3bot in Telegram
3. Tap Start and follow the instructions
4. The bot will confirm your tag is linked to your account
4
Activate your emergency contacts
For each person listed as your emergency contact:
1. Ask them to download Telegram
2. Have them message @innovat3bot and tap Start
3. They will automatically receive GPS alerts whenever your tag is tapped
5
Keep your profile up to date
If your medical info, contacts, or photo ever change, contact us at info@innovat3.co.za or WhatsApp +27 72 588 3875 and we'll update your card. Your tag ID stays the same forever.
