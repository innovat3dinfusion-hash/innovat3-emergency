2Information We Collect

We collect and process the following categories of personal information:

  • Identity Information: First name, last name, date of birth, profile photograph
  • Contact Information: Email address, mobile phone number, Telegram handle, home address
  • Emergency Contacts: Names, relationships, phone numbers, WhatsApp and Telegram details of up to three nominated contacts
  • Medical Information: Blood type, medical conditions and allergies, current medications, medical aid name and membership number (see Section 3)
  • Service Data: Subscription plan, Tag ID, Tag colour preferences, delivery address
  • Technical Data: GPS coordinates, location address, device scan timestamps, IP address (via GitHub Pages logs)
  • Legal Consent Data: Date and time of consent to these Terms

3Special Personal Information

⚠️Medical and health information constitutes Special Personal Information under POPIA Section 26. We may only process this information with your explicit, informed consent.

By registering for the Service and ticking the relevant consent checkbox, you explicitly consent to innovat3. collecting and processing your health and medical information, including blood type, medical conditions, allergies, and medications, for the sole purpose of displaying this information to Finders and emergency responders in a medical emergency situation.

You may withdraw this consent at any time by contacting us, however this will result in the deactivation of your emergency profile and termination of the Service.

4How We Collect Information

We collect your personal information through the following means:

  • Registration Form: Information you provide directly when registering for the Service
  • Tag Scans: GPS coordinates and device information captured automatically when your Tag is scanned
  • Communications: Information provided when you contact our support team
  • Subscription Management: Payment information processed through our payment gateway (we do not store card details)

5Purpose of Processing

We process your personal information strictly for the following lawful purposes:

  • Delivering the innovat3. Smart NFC Emergency Tag Service as described in the Terms and Conditions
  • Displaying your emergency profile to Finders who scan your Tag
  • Sending GPS location alerts to your nominated emergency contacts via Telegram and WhatsApp
  • Maintaining your subscription account and processing payments
  • Communicating with you regarding your subscription, service updates, and support
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations under South African law
  • Improving the Service through aggregated, anonymised usage analytics

We will not use your personal information for any purpose not listed above without your explicit prior consent.

6Public Disclosure of Your Emergency Profile

ℹ️Your emergency profile is intentionally publicly accessible. This is the core function of the Service. Any person who scans your NFC Tag will be able to view your profile information.

By subscribing to the Service, you explicitly acknowledge and consent that your emergency profile — including your name, date of birth, blood type, medical conditions, medications, home address, and emergency contact details — will be visible to any person who scans your Tag, without requiring a password or authentication.

This public disclosure is the essential mechanism by which the Service delivers its emergency identification function. If you do not consent to this public disclosure, you should not register for the Service.

7Sharing with Third Parties

We share your personal information only with the following categories of third parties, and only to the extent necessary to deliver the Service:

  • Google LLC: We store your registration data and scan logs on Google Sheets (Google Cloud infrastructure). Google processes data as a sub-processor in accordance with applicable data protection laws.
  • GitHub Inc.: Your emergency profile web page is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub processes limited technical data as described in its privacy policy.
  • Telegram Messenger: Emergency alert notifications are sent to your nominated contacts via the Telegram API. Message content includes your name, GPS location, and basic medical information.
  • WhatsApp LLC: We generate pre-formatted WhatsApp message links to facilitate contact between the Finder and your nominated contacts. We do not directly access WhatsApp APIs or store WhatsApp messages.
  • imgbb: If you upload a profile photograph, it is stored on imgbb's servers and the public URL is included in your emergency profile.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

8Cross-Border Data Transfers

Some of our third-party service providers process data outside the Republic of South Africa, specifically:

  • Google LLC — servers located globally including the United States and European Union
  • GitHub Inc. — servers located in the United States
  • Telegram — servers located in multiple jurisdictions globally
  • imgbb — servers located outside South Africa

By registering for the Service, you consent to your personal information being transferred to and processed in countries outside South Africa. innovat3. takes reasonable steps to ensure these third parties maintain appropriate data protection standards. Where reasonably practicable, we rely on providers that comply with GDPR or equivalent international standards.

9Data Security

innovat3. implements reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, alteration, or disclosure, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit between your device and our platforms
  • Access controls limiting who within innovat3. can access subscriber data
  • Use of industry-standard cloud providers with their own security certifications
  • Regular review of access permissions and security practices

No system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach that is likely to harm you, innovat3. will notify you and the Information Regulator as required by POPIA within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.

10Data Retention

We retain your personal information for the following periods:

  • Active Subscribers: For the duration of your active subscription
  • Lapsed Subscriptions: Profile data retained for 90 days after lapse to allow reactivation, then deleted unless required by law
  • Scan Logs: GPS scan logs retained for 24 months after subscription end date for dispute resolution purposes, then permanently deleted
  • Financial Records: Payment records retained for 5 years as required by South African tax law

Upon receiving a verified deletion request, we will delete your personal information within 30 business days, subject to any legal retention obligations.

12Cookies & Tracking

Our emergency profile web pages and registration form do not use cookies for tracking or marketing purposes. Limited session data may be processed by GitHub Pages as part of standard web hosting.

GPS location data is captured only at the moment a Tag is scanned and only with the device's built-in location permission prompt. No background location tracking occurs.

13Children's Privacy

The parent or legal guardian is responsible for ensuring the accuracy of information provided for any minor subscriber and consents to the processing of the minor's personal information as described in this Policy.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors without parental consent.
