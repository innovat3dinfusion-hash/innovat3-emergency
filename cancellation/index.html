Refund & Cancellation Policy

Clear, fair terms for subscriptions, hardware, and refunds — governed by South African law.

📅 Effective: 14 April 2026 📍 Governed by South African Law 🔄 Applies to all Innovat3 subscribers
✅
Your rights matter. As a South African consumer you are protected by the Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008 (CPA). This policy is written to comply fully with the CPA and POPIA. If anything is unclear, contact us — we will always act fairly.

1Subscription Cancellation

Your Innovat3 subscription is a monthly recurring service. You may cancel at any time, for any reason, with no cancellation fee.

  • Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You will not be charged again after the cycle in which you cancel.
  • If you cancel mid-month, you retain access to your active tag and digital profile until the end of the period you have already paid for.
  • No partial-month refunds are issued for unused days in a current billing period, unless the 5-day cooling-off period applies (see Section 2).
  • Annual plans (if offered) may be cancelled within the 5-day cooling-off period for a full refund, or at any time for a pro-rata refund of unused complete months, minus the hardware cost.
ℹ️
To cancel, simply contact us via WhatsApp or email (see Section 8). We do not require a written letter or any reason for cancellation.

25-Day Cooling-Off Period (CPA)

Under the Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008, Section 16, you have the right to cancel any direct-marketing or electronically concluded agreement within 5 (five) business days of the transaction, without penalty and without giving a reason.

✅
If you cancel within 5 business days of your first payment: You are entitled to a full refund of the subscription fee paid. The physical tag must be returned to us in its original, undamaged condition. Return courier costs are at the subscriber's expense unless the tag was defective.

To exercise your cooling-off right, contact us at support@innovat3.co.za or via WhatsApp within the 5-business-day window. We will process your refund within 10 business days of receiving the returned tag (if applicable).

3Hardware (Physical Tag) Refunds

The once-off hardware cost (physical NFC/QR safety tag) charged at registration is separate from the recurring subscription fee.

  • Defective tag: If your tag arrives damaged or defective, we will replace it at no cost. Contact us within 7 days of receipt with a photo of the defect.
  • Tag not received: If your tag has not arrived within 10 business days of your order, contact us. We will re-ship or refund the hardware cost in full.
  • Change of mind: Hardware refunds are not available after the 5-day cooling-off period unless the item is defective, as per CPA Section 55 (goods must be reasonably suitable for their purpose).
  • Lost tag: Replacement tags may be purchased. Pricing is available on request. Your digital profile and subscription remain active — only the physical tag is replaced.
⚠️
Hardware refunds require the physical tag to be returned to Innovat3 before a refund is processed, unless the item is confirmed defective or undelivered.

4Failed or Declined Payments

Innovat3 uses PayFast for all recurring billing. PayFast will automatically retry a failed payment.

  • If a payment fails, your subscription will enter a grace period of 7 days. Your tag will remain active during this period.
  • If the payment is not resolved within 7 days, your subscription will be suspended. Your data is retained for 30 days to allow reactivation.
  • To reactivate a suspended subscription, visit the Reactivation page on the Innovat3 website.
  • Innovat3 is not liable for fees charged by your bank for declined debit orders.

5How to Cancel or Request a Refund

All cancellations and refund requests are handled directly by Innovat3. The process is simple:

  1. 1
    Contact us via WhatsApp, email, or the contact form on our website (details in Section 8). State your full name and the email address used to register.
  2. 2
    Confirm your request. For refunds, state the reason. For cooling-off cancellations, no reason is required.
  3. 3
    Return the hardware (if applicable — cancellations within 5 days or defective tags). We will provide a return address.
  4. 4
    Refund processed within 10 business days of confirming cancellation (and receiving returned hardware where applicable). Refunds are made to the original payment method.
ℹ️
We aim to acknowledge all cancellation and refund requests within 1 business day.

6What Happens After Cancellation

  • Your recurring debit order is cancelled immediately with PayFast — no further charges will occur after your current billing period ends.
  • Your digital tag profile (the data shown when someone scans your tag) remains accessible until the end of your paid period.
  • After your subscription ends, your tag will display a "subscription inactive" message to anyone who scans it.
  • Your personal data is retained for 30 days after cancellation to allow reactivation. After 30 days, data is deleted in accordance with our Privacy Policy and POPIA requirements.
  • The physical tag becomes inactive (will no longer link to emergency contact information) once the subscription ends.

7Exclusions

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Subscription fees for months already billed and used (beyond the 5-day cooling-off period).
  • Hardware costs after the 5-day cooling-off period, unless the tag is defective or undelivered.
  • Fees arising from your own failure to update payment details, causing a lapse in service.
  • Any loss or damage arising from reliance on the Innovat3 service in an emergency situation — the service is a communication aid, not a guarantee of emergency response.
⚠️
Innovat3 Safety Tags is a supplementary safety tool. It does not replace emergency services, medical care, or parental supervision. Always call 10111 (SAPS) or 10177 (Ambulance) in an emergency.
